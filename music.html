I play bass and synths as Silent Reading.

I'm the founder and arranger for a number of bands of varying levels of novelty, performing overly ambitious and underrehearsed esoterica. Previous nights include:

  • MeatBute: A Tribute to Meat Loaf
  • Enchantment Under the Sea — playing mid-century tiki music/exotica
  • Fannheim Steamroller — an annual Christmas in July spectacular
  • The Bookhouse Boys — playing the music of David Lynch
  • Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes — the Mos Eisley Cantina band
  • Surf du Soleil
