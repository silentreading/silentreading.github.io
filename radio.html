I'm a Multimedia Producer for Studio 360 from PRI in association with Slate. Before that, I produced Sound Opinions from WBEZ Chicago. I've also DJ'd for CHIRP Radio and WHPK in Chicago.

Highlights of Recent Features I've Produced

  • C.W. McCall's "Convoy" — I report on the strange story of C.W. McCall's novelty trucker hit "Convoy," involving hamburger buns, conflict in the Middle East, labor unrest, and Christmas music.
  • Billy Bragg on Skiffle — My interview with Billy Bragg about how the short-lived 1950s British craze shaped modern pop music.
  • Rickenbacker Electric 12-String Guitar — the history of the iconic instrument's use, from The Beatles to jangle pop and beyond.
  • Mavis Staples — an hour-long restropective with the Civil Rights icon and gospel legend.
  • World Tour: Brazil — an introduction to Brazil's musical history, focusing on the crucial decade of 1958-1968.
  • Pet Sounds — Classic Album Dissection of The Beach Boys' masterpiece.
  • Rock & the Environment — exploring music's ecological impact with environmentalist Bill McKibben.
